About

Kit Wagstaff | @kitwagstafftattoo

Kit Wagstaff has worked as a professional tattoo artist for 3 years. She is currently tattooing at Inkabella Tattoo Studio in Barry, South Glamorgan.
When it comes to style, Kit enjoys detailed, illustrative tattoos with stipple shading and a side of traditional/blackwork.
Kit doesn't just create tattoos; she devotes her life to the craft. Constantly creating new designs and developing her techniques.
If you're looking for a skilled tattoo artist who puts her clients first, then you have come to the right place. Everyone is welcome.
